Our Services

We offer a wide range of medical and healthcare services:

Emergency Services (24/7)

Immediate care for urgent medical conditions with professional staff on standby.

Outpatient Department (OPD)

Consultations, check-ups, and minor procedures without hospital admission.

Laboratory and Diagnostic Services

Comprehensive lab tests and diagnostic imaging for accurate medical results.

Pharmacy Services

On-site pharmacy providing prescribed medications and over-the-counter drugs.

Inpatient Rooms and Wards

Comfortable and safe rooms for patients requiring hospital admission.

Radiology (X-Ray, Ultrasound)

Advanced imaging services to assist in diagnosis and treatment plans.

General Consultation

Comprehensive health consultations with our experienced doctors and staff.
