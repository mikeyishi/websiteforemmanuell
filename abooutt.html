The Department of Medical Education and Research is responsible for the proper training, credentialing, and documentation of all postgraduate medical trainees.

Trainings focus on safe and appropriate patient care while supporting the professional and personal development of trainees through proper supervision.

The department also promotes community health programs such as vaccination drives and medical outreach activities.

Vision
Center of excellence in medical education, training, and research.
Mission
Train and develop competent and compassionate house staff.
Foster education, training, clinical research, and continuous professional development of medical staff.
Utilize modern internet facilities in acquiring global medical information.
Promote knowledge sharing with other healthcare institutions.
