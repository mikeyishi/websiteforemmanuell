Trainings

The Department of Medical Education and Research is responsible for proper training, credentialing, and documentation of all postgraduate medical trainees. It oversees quality educational trainings for all levels.

Trainings focus on safe patient care and professional development with supervision from qualified healthcare practitioners.

The Department also organizes health activities and community outreach programs, including vaccination and medical awareness programs.

Vision

Center of excellence in medical education, trainings, and research.

Mission

  • Train and develop competent and compassionate house staff.
  • Foster education, training, clinical research, and continuous professional growth.
  • Utilize modern facilities and internet resources for global medical knowledge.
  • Promote knowledge exchange and collaboration with other institutions.
