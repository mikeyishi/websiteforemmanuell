About Our Health Services

Cancer Institute: Provides comprehensive cancer care including diagnostics, chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and patient support.

Institute of Urology: Specialized in treating urinary tract disorders, kidney stones, and urological surgeries with advanced equipment.

Cardiology Center: Focused on heart health, offering diagnostics, interventions, and rehabilitation for cardiovascular patients.

Maternity & Childcare: Comprehensive prenatal, delivery, and pediatric care with experienced specialists.

We aim to provide state-of-the-art facilities and expert care for all patients in a safe and comfortable environment.
