Parking Guide
  • Parking is free for the first 15 minutes.
  • 24-hour parking available at Medical Arts Building.
  • Valet services available from 6AM to 6PM.
  • Lost or damaged parking cards will have a fee.
  • Free shuttle services available between buildings.
Chapel

Our chapel is located on the sixth floor of Building 1.

Masses are held Monday–Saturday at 12:00 noon, Saturday at 5:00 pm, and Sunday at 5:00 pm.

Confession available Monday–Saturday from 11:30 am to 12:30 pm.
